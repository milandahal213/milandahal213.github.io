Connecting to Motors

You can use Python to send commands to a motor.

Connect to a LEGO Education Science Single Motor

  1. Press the power button to turn it on.
  2. Press the power button again to set it to Bluetooth mode. The motor should beep.
  3. Click "Connect" in the "Bluetoothâ„¢" section on the top-right of this page.
  4. Select your motor and click "Pair."

Type the lines below into the REPL to send Python instructions to your motor.

  • motor.set_speed(50)
  • motor.run()
  • motor.stop()
