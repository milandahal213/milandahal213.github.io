Meet the REPL

A REPL is like a live chat with your computer. For practice or troubleshooting, programmers can type one line of code at a time and instantly see the result.

Try typing these lines one-by-one into the REPL on the right. Press [ENTER] after each line to see the result.

  • 1 + 1

The REPL remembers what you've previously typed. Try defining a variable. Then use the variable in a math problem.

  • a = 2
  • 2 + a
