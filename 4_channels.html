Channels

Another powerful part of Python is the ability to interact on channels. Python channels allow two webpages to exchange messages and data.

Hit the connect button to enable the channels.

You can use the text box (send this) to type your message and hit the send button to send message.

or you can use the REPL to send messages.

  • channel.send("Hello there!!")

Try sending something else!

We will use channels to collaborate across groups.
