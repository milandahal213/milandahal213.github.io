Connecting to LEGO Hardware

Connect this page to the LEGO Education Science single motor.

  • Power single motor on and press power button once to advertise via bluetooth. Press the "Connect" button to connect.
  • Name the connected motor by replacing "element" with "motor"

You can control the motor with Python commands in the REPL.

  • motor.set_speed(50)
    motor.run()
  • motor.stop()

Try changing the speed of the motor.
